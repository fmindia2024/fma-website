The Identity

Legacy of Juan Suárez Botas

The FMA identity is a tribute to the late Juan Suárez Botas (1958–1992), a visionary Spanish architect and illustrator whose work bridged the gap between rigorous structure and artistic fluidity.

Known for his contributions to the "La Movida Madrileña" and his architectural illustrations in New York, Botas possessed a unique ability to humanize geometry. Our logo, an original sketch by Botas, embodies this philosophy: the heavy, grounded architectural forms are softened by his signature energetic, hand-drawn lines.

It serves as a constant reminder that architecture is not just about engineering, but about the spirited mark of the human hand.
