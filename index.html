Our identity is more than a symbol; it is a piece of history. Crafted by the renowned artist K.M. Vasudevan Namboodiri, the logo captures the essence of our architectural philosophy—where tradition intersects with modernity.

The seemingly fluid lines juxtaposed against rigid geometry represent the dialogue between nature and structure, a core tenet of our design language.
